Nadezda Beletskaya

Malmö

I work with soft pastels, watercolours and ink, acrylic.

The vivid and soft hues of watercolours. The preciseness and streamlined features of ink. I have always been fascinated by both of these and the way I can make them both come to live and develop together in my paintings. My art is not only a fusion of these two motifs, but also a journey that unites the classical art school with Chinese painting.

“Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.” Leonardo da Vinci
